APTUS GANA

    APTUS     -   Aptitude (In Latin)
    GANA     -   Group of members working
together for an aim (In Sanskrit)

         Our Club Objectives :

             ⋙ To improve quality of aptitude level among the students
             ⋙ To prepare them for Aptitude exams for campus placements
             ⋙ It helps in bringing out students strengths, talents
             ⋙ It improves the students non-divergent thinking
             ⋙ To enlighten the spark of logical thinking in students
             ⋙ To be a source of inspiration & encouragement

         Activites Proposed :

             ⋙ Aptitude test for students at different levels
             ⋙ Numerical or mathematical ability
             ⋙ Abstract reasoning
             ⋙ Perceptual speed and accuracy
             ⋙ Quiz Competitions: College Level , Inter College Level
             ⋙ Exhibitions -Exhibiting student's talents in various fields
